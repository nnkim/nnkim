Enter Score and Get Grade

Code that return grades when enter scores.


Make a program that inputs a student's score and returns grade.
Condition :
함수의 입력은 정수형으로 받고, 함수의 반환은 문자열로 반환 입력된 성적은 점수 구간에 따라 계산됨

grade range:
From scores 90 to 100 are A
From scores below 90 to 80 and above are B
From scores below 80 to 70 and above are C
From scores below 70 to 60 and above are D
From scores below 60 are F
